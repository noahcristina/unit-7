SPY Chart by TradingView


SPY otherwise known as the S&P 500 is the biggest ETF (exchange traded fund) in the world. It holds generally the top 500 biggest US publicly traded companies. It is used as a general indicator to see how the US stock market is preforming.

ETF are basically index funds that track the market and are usually recommended over mutual funds as they tend to have lower management fees and are more tax effective.

If someone is looking to invest into equities, SPY is a good choice to look into for people wanting low fees and diversification.
